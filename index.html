Alexander Hepburn

About

PhD student at University of Bristol in Engineering Mathematics department. I began my PhD in September 2017 after graduating from MEng Engineering Mathematics in June 2017. My PhD supervisors are Raul Santos-Rodriguez and Ryan McConville

Research Interests

I am interesting in machine learning, deep learning, music information retreival and cost-sensitive learning.

SICA for Music

Subjectively Interesting Component Analysis for projections of musical features.

Proper Losses for Learning with Example-Dependent Costs

Framework for proper losses in example-dependent cost functions applied to credit score and musical genre classification. Published in ECML-LIDTA 2018

Album Cover Generation from Genre Tags

Generating album covers using GANs. A dataset containing album covers and musical features also constructed using the Spotify API. Publish in 10th International Workshop on Machine Learning and Music
